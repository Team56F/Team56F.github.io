Overveiw

Team 56F's robot at a glance.

Mechanical Design

• 1:1 gear ratio for a balance of torque and speed
• Simple chain leveled lift
• Scorpion claw for fast manipulation of game elements.
• Unique pneumatic mobile goal lift for fast manipulation of goals.
• High gear ratio on the lift, No rubber bands needed.
• Simple, effective autonmous that works on timing, not sensors.
• Easy maintenance and repair by design. No fussing with tight corners.
• Tested, reliable design. This robot has driven about 10-15 hours with no major failures.
• Compatable. By design, this robot can work with just about any other.

Autonomous and Extras

• Retreives 1 mobie goal and drops it off in 5 point zone
• Builds upon last year's robot and improves issues with it
• Battery life is not an issue in autonmous performance
